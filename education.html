St.Andrews College

My education began at St.Andrews College. I started from the beginning. From Junior Infants all the way to graduating. I began secondary school in 2003 and graduated in 2009. For the Leaving Certificate I studied the core subjects of Maths(O), Irish(O), English(H) and my option classes were Business(H), Classical Studies(H), Geography(H), German(H) and Biology(H). I got 395 points in my Leaving Certificate. While at St.Andrews I was Head Prefect of my form. I also won many sporting prizes. In hockey I won the All-Ireland schools competition in 2008 and won 2 Senior Leagues. In Football I was twice named Player of the tournament for the schools football competition.



U.C.D

From 2009 until 2014 I studied Arts at University College Dublin. I graduated in 2014 with a joint major in Geography and Greek & Roman studies. I also studied other modules such as: Information Studies, Philosophy, Economics and Computer technology. My concentrations in Geography and Greek & Roman were: Political Geography, Urban & Regional Planning, Geographic Analysis, European Integration, Geographic Research Techniques, Ideas In Geography, Archeology of Athens, Archeology of Rome and Greek Tragedy and Myth. While in UCD I won the Hockey Colours in 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014.

D.I.T

I am currently studying a Pre-Masters in Computing at Dublin Institute of Technology. I am also on a hockey scholarship from DIT. My course in computing currently consists of five main modules: Web & User Interface Design, Object Oriented Programming, Information Systems, System Analysis & Testing and Architecture, Operating Systems and Networks. There are many interesting and good learning outcomes to these modules. Being able to code in HTML and CSS for web design, while being able to code Python for programming. On the Hockey side, in October DIT finished runners up to UU in the hockey varsities.
