Hockey

I have been playing Hockey since I was 12 years old. I was very keen on football and had played that until I was 15. When I went into secondary school in St.Andrews College I got much more into hockey and joined my first club team called Pembroke Wanderers. I played with them for 5 years until I was 17 and then join Corinthian Hockey Club in Rathfarnham. It was here that I won my first cap for Ireland. After 5 years with Corinthians I moved to Monkstown Hockey Club where most of my friends play their hockey. I am now in my 4th year with Monkstown HC and have won numerous titles with them. I hope to continue this in the future. With Ireland I have had a good period of success. I have been involved with the squad since I was 19 until just recently in March when I pulled out due to college and work commitments.



Club Hockey

I have played for three different clubs so far in my Hockey Career. I first joined Pembroke HC. I played underage Hockey there until I was 17. I then moved onto Corinthian Hockey Club where I played from 2008 until 2013. While there I won the Leinster Senior Cup and was Young Junior Player of the year in 2008 and was nominated for Irish young player of the year in 2009. I was top scorer for the club in 2009, 2010 and 2011. From 2013 onwards I have played for Monkstown HC. I have won many trophies with them which include: 3 X IHL Winners, 1 X Irish Senior Cup Winner,2 X Leinster Senior League Winner and 2 X Leinster Senior Cup Winner. I was top scorer for Monkstown in the 2013/2014 season. I have also represented Monkstown in the EHL twice, in Barcelona and Hamburg. The premier European hockey competition.

Ireland Hockey

I have represented my Country 32 times to date in Hockey. Since being involved in 2009 I have won many titles and traveled all over the world while playing. I got my first Cap in 2011 against Canada. From there I have represented Ireland at the Celtic Cup where I have won it three times. I have also been runner up to England in the NOW Pensions Cup and represented Ireland at the Hockey World League in New Dehli, India in 2013. We finished second there, runners up to the hosts India. I was also part of the team runners up to South Africa in the Tri-Nations Series in Dublin. I have scored 9 goals for my country including a hat-rick against Scotland in the Celtic Cup 2012. I Recently pulled out of the squad in march thi year to focus on work and study. With the team qualifying for the Rio Olympics I will be pushing for a place back in the squad.
