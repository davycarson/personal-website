HayFever Relief

I am currently working along side my dad in creating a self-help and treatment App for people who suffer from Hayfever. Dr.Paul Carson is the leading doctor in europe for Allergies and Hayfever. I came to him with the idea in the summer and we are now currently in the wireframe stages to mockup stages. We are working in situ with Tapadoo and the Science Picture Company to bring a world class App to the market. Due for release in March/April, it is very exciting times for this venture.

GrantLodge

Grantlodge organizes and runs the elections for any referendum for Dublin City Constituencies. Usually based in the RDS or City West hotel, I worked worked twice for the company in 2011 and more recently in 2015 (Mariage referendum). I was a computer operator in 2011, creating and designing labels for over 400 polling stations and over 600 ballot boxes that would be used in the election for voting.

Southside Sports

From September to June, starting last year I worked for Southside Sports. Southside Sports is a coaching company providing multi-sport programmes to schools in south Dublin and Wicklow. My role was head hockey coach as well as teaching PE to children in schools. The company looks after over 15 different schools in the Dublin and Wicklow area. My job was implement training sessions for schools involved in hockey and organize matches and tournaments. Along with teaching PE to children during the day.
